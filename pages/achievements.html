Achievements of Ahmed Abu Zeid

Ahmed Abu Zeid has made a significant impact as an educator, content creator, and digital skills trainer. His journey from engineering to online education has inspired millions. Below are some of his notable achievements.

Key Achievements

  • Founder of "Droos Online" - Launched in 2012, now one of the most popular Arabic YouTube educational channels with millions of subscribers.
  • Massive YouTube Growth - Over 7 million subscribers and hundreds of millions of views on educational content.
  • Impact on Online Learning - Helped thousands of students improve their English, freelancing skills, and self-development.
  • Recognized in the Educational Community - Featured in various media and conferences discussing digital education.
  • Social Media Influence - Built a strong presence on multiple platforms like Telegram, Facebook, and Instagram, engaging with millions of followers.

Awards & Recognition

  • Recognized as a top Arabic educational content creator.
  • Invited to major conferences and events on online education.
  • Featured in various media platforms for his impact on digital learning.

Future Aspirations

Ahmed Abu Zeid continues to expand his educational content, aiming to reach more learners worldwide. His future plans include developing structured courses and expanding his digital platforms.

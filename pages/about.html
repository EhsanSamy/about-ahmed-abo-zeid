Ahmed-Abu-Zeid

Content Creator | Educator | Digital Skills Trainer

A passionate content creator and educator, Ahmed Abu Zeid is the founder of Droos Online, one of the most popular Arabic YouTube channels for self-improvement, productivity, and language learning. With a deep love for technology, education, and personal development, he helps thousands of viewers enhance their skills in English, freelancing, and digital tools. His engaging and informative videos inspire learners to grow both personally and professionally.

The Early Years

Ahmed Abu Zeid was born in September 1992 in Egypt. From an early age, he exhibited a passion for learning and technology, which later influenced his journey into content creation and education.

Educational Journey

In 2009, Ahmed enrolled in the Faculty of Engineering at Tanta University, initially aspiring to study petroleum engineering. However, due to circumstances, he pursued civil engineering instead. During his studies, he developed an interest in computer-related fields, which later played a significant role in his career.

Career Breakthrough

Ahmed's journey into content creation began in 2012 while he was still a student. Noticing a lack of Arabic educational content online, he started a YouTube channel named "Droos Online" to provide tutorials on software like Photoshop and After Effects. After graduating in 2014, he worked as a civil engineer in Marsa Alam but continued to nurture his passion for content creation. In 2017, he made the pivotal decision to become a full-time YouTuber. His channel expanded to cover topics such as English language learning, productivity, and self-development, amassing nearly 7 million subscribers by 2022.
